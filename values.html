What I stand for

iHeel® runs on three values. They decide how every pad is made, packed and supported. Here is what they mean in practice.

Value 01

Care

Every pad that leaves my workshop is tested by hand. Not a sample from the batch: every batch, every time. If I wouldn't wear it, I won't ship it.

Care doesn't stop at the product. Questions go straight to me, not a ticket queue. Instructions come in your language, and packaging is 100% plastic-free because caring about your heels shouldn't cost the planet.

Value 02

Quality

Most heel pads fail because factories cut too many per press, trimming away the fused edges that hold the layers together. I refuse to work that way.

iHeel® pads are produced in small batches with precision-cut edges and premium cushioning materials. That is why one pad lasts months, while cheap alternatives quit within days.

Value 03

Innovation

iHeel® exists because I refused to accept the standard way of making heel pads. In 2022 I re-engineered the whole process from scratch: new cutting method, better materials, stricter testing.

And it doesn't stop there. Every batch teaches me something, and every improvement goes straight into the next production run. The pad you buy today is the best one I have ever made.
