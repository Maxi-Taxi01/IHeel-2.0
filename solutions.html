What iHeel® solves

One pad, four everyday shoe problems. Here is what customers use iHeel® for, and how it works for each.

Your heel slips out of the shoe

A snugger fit for loose shoes

Shoes that are half a size too big, or leather that has stretched, let your heel ride up with every step. The pad adds a soft layer exactly where the gap is, so the shoe holds your heel the way it should.

New shoes rub and cause blisters

Break in new shoes without the pain

That slow burn at the back of your heel in the first weeks of a new pair? The pad covers the rubbing zone with a soft surface, so you can wear new shoes all day from day one.

Sore heels after long days

Comfort for work days on your feet

Standing or walking for hours makes stiff heel counters press and chafe. The cushioning softens every step and stays in place, whether you wear heels, flats or work shoes.

The heel lining wears through

Protect the shoes you love

Friction does not only hurt your heel, it slowly eats the lining of your favourite pair. The pad shields the lining from wear, so the shoes you love last longer.
