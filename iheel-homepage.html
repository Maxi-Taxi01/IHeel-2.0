The story behind iHeel®

Your pain was my pain too.

"Every heel pad I bought failed within days. So in 2022 I re-engineered the whole process myself — small batches, precision-cut edges, zero compromise."
— Rik, founder of iHeel®, the Netherlands

Most pads fail because factories cut too many per press, trimming off the fused edges that hold the layers together. I refuse to work that way. Every batch is small. Every pad is tested. If I wouldn't wear it, I won't ship it.
